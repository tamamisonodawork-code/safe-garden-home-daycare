About Safe Garden Home Daycare

A warm daycare where your child is truly known

Safe Garden Home Daycare is a licensed multi-age home daycare in Coquitlam where children are cared for in a small, calm, and family-like environment.

Because our setting is small, children are not just supervised. They are seen, supported, and gently guided through daily routines, play-based learning, outdoor time, and warm relationships.

Families choose Safe Garden because they want more than childcare. They want a place where their child can feel safe, known, happy, and ready to grow.

Children are also offered a nutritious breakfast and afternoon snacks, with thoughtful choices such as fruit, homemade items when possible, and limited white sugar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers for Coquitlam daycare families.

Do you currently have available spots?

Spaces are limited in our small-group daycare, and availability can change depending on age, schedule, and enrollment. Families are welcome to join the waiting list for future openings.

Can we book a tour before a spot is available?

Yes, tours can be arranged before a future opening becomes available. Joining the waiting list is still the best first step, and we can re-confirm details when a suitable spot gets closer.

How do I join the waiting list?

Please complete the waiting list form on this page. We will contact you by phone or email when a suitable spot becomes available.

What ages do you accept?

We generally care for children around 18 months to 5 years old, depending on the child’s readiness, group balance, and licensing requirements.

Do you offer full-time or part-time care?

Safe Garden mainly offers full-time care. Any part-time availability depends on the current enrollment, age fit, and schedule. Please join the waiting list or contact us to ask about current availability.

Do you participate in CCFRI?

Yes. Safe Garden participates in the Child Care Fee Reduction Initiative for eligible licensed child care spaces. Families do not need to apply for CCFRI separately.

How many children do you care for?

Safe Garden is a licensed multi-age home daycare for up to 8 children. The small-group setting allows children to receive personal attention in a calm, family-like environment.

Will there be a support teacher?

Safe Garden is owner-led, and a support teacher is present for special events, celebrations, and busier days. Daily support depends on the number of children, the day’s schedule, and program needs.

What food and snacks do you provide?

Safe Garden provides breakfast and afternoon snacks with a focus on nutritious choices, fruit, homemade items when possible, organic options when available, and limited white sugar. Families provide lunch, diapers, wipes, and personal items.

Do you provide daily updates?

Yes. Families receive daily Lillio updates, including photos, meals, naps, activities, and special moments.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow a play-based curriculum that supports social-emotional development, language and literacy, cognitive development, physical growth, creativity, independence, and confidence.

Do children have outdoor play time?

Yes. Weather permitting, children enjoy outdoor play each day. Outdoor time supports physical activity, confidence, healthy growth, and joyful exploration.

Is Safe Garden Home Daycare bilingual?

Safe Garden Home Daycare provides English-based care with French support and exposure. Chonon is fluent in English and French, and children may hear French through simple words, songs, and daily routines.

Do you serve families from Burnaby, Port Coquitlam, or Port Moody?

Yes. Safe Garden Home Daycare is located in Coquitlam near the Lougheed and Burquitlam area and may be a convenient option for families from nearby communities such as Burnaby, Port Moody, and Port Coquitlam, depending on location, commute, and availability.

Do you celebrate birthdays and special days?

Yes. Safe Garden celebrates special days such as Easter, Canada Day, Christmas, seasonal activities, and birthdays. Seasonal events and daycare-planned celebrations are prepared by the daycare. Birthday celebrations can be arranged when requested by parents; families provide any fruit, treats, or decorations they would like to include. Birthday celebrations are generally for the children only, and a trusted support teacher is present for celebration days and busier events.

Where can families read reviews for Safe Garden Home Daycare?

Families can read Google reviews for Safe Garden Home Daycare on our Google Business Profile. We are grateful for feedback from families and continue to provide safe, warm, and nurturing childcare in Coquitlam.
