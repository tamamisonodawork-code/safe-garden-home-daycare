About Safe Garden Home Daycare

Licensed, affordable home daycare in Coquitlam.

Safe Garden Home Daycare is a multi-age licensed home daycare in Coquitlam, BC for children around 18 months to 5 years old. Our owner-led program is guided by Chonon, a Certified Early Childhood Educator with Canadian daycare experience.

We provide small-group care for up to 8 children in a safe, nurturing, family-like environment. Children are encouraged to play, collaborate, and learn through a play-based curriculum that supports social-emotional development, language and literacy, cognitive development, physical growth, creativity, and independence.

Safe Garden participates in the Child Care Fee Reduction Initiative to help eligible families access more affordable childcare in Coquitlam.

Our daycare is located on Denton Street in Coquitlam, close to Lougheed Town Centre and Burquitlam Station, and may be convenient for families from Port Moody, Burnaby, and Port Coquitlam depending on commute and availability.

Meet the owner

Meet Chonon, Owner of Safe Garden Home Daycare

Chonon is a Certified Early Childhood Educator with a certificate in Early Childhood Education and Teaching from Stenberg College. Before opening Safe Garden Home Daycare, a licensed home daycare in Coquitlam, she gained hands-on experience working in Canadian daycare settings.

Her background in early childhood education, daycare experience, and previous law firm work helps her provide patient, thoughtful, and family-like care.

She welcomes families from different cultures and backgrounds, and offers English-based care with natural French exposure through simple words, songs, and daily routines.
